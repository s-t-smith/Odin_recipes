Green Bean Bundles

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of whole green beans
  • 1 pound of bacon, cut in half (make shorter strips)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt (or crushed garlic cloves)

Equipment:

  • Knife and a cutting board
  • Glass caserole dish or Pyrex pan
  • Toothpicks

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Snap ends of green beans. Keep lengths as similar as possible.
  3. Group 4 to 6 beans together and roll them in a strip of bacon. Pierce the bundle with a toothpick to keep it together.
  4. Place bundles flat in the caserole dish or Pyrex pan.
  5. Mix melted butter, brown sugar, and garlic together and pour over the bundles.
  6. Cover pan in foil and place in oven. Bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and continue baking for 5 to 15 minutes to finish the bacon and remove excess liquid.
  8. Serve from caserole dish to get the most out of the au ju.
